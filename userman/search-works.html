Searching for Works

WordHoard provides a simple bibliographic search tool for finding works in its database.

The "Find Works" command is found in the "Find" menu (Command-L). It presents a simple form where you may enter any combination of author, title, or publication date range, using asterisk as a wild card.

The search result can be grouped by a variety of criteria. These groupings can also be dragged into the query panel of the Searching for Lemmas tool to form search criteria for lemma searching. Works can also be dragged into Work Set windows for saving as Work Sets.
